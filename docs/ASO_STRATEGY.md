## App Store Product Page Experiments

### Experiment 1: Hero Screenshot Focus
**Hypothesis**: Leading with AI predictions converts better than "fast logging"
**Control**: Screenshot 1 = Speed/2-tap logging
**Variant A**: Screenshot 1 = AI nap prediction
**Metric**: Conversion rate (page view to install)
**Duration**: 2 weeks or 500 installs per variant

### Experiment 2: Subtitle Messaging
**Hypothesis**: "AI" in subtitle increases conversion
**Control**: "Smart Feeding & Sleep Log"
**Variant A**: "AI Nap Predictions & Logging"
**Variant B**: "Track in 2 Taps + AI Insights"
**Metric**: Conversion rate
**Duration**: 2 weeks

### Experiment 3: Icon Variations
**Hypothesis**: A warmer, friendlier icon increases installs
**Control**: Current teal icon
**Variant A**: Gradient teal-to-purple
**Variant B**: Add small sparkle/star element
**Metric**: Conversion rate
**Duration**: 3 weeks

## Review & Rating Strategy

### In-App Review Triggers
1. **After first "aha moment"**: First accurate nap prediction confirmed
2. **Milestone celebrations**: 7-day streak, 100 logs
3. **After positive feedback**: User taps thumbs-up on prediction
4. **Never after**: Crashes, errors, failed syncs, negative feedback

### Review Prompt Copy
> "You've been tracking for 7 days! üéâ If Nuzzle is helping you, would you mind leaving a review? It helps other parents find us."

### Responding to Reviews
- Respond to ALL 1-2 star reviews within 24 hours
- Thank positive reviewers if they mention specific features
- Never be defensive; always be helpful
- Offer direct support email for issues

## Tracking & Analytics

### Key ASO Metrics to Monitor

4. **Keyword Rankings** - Position for target keywords
