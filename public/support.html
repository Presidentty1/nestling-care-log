Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my subscription?

In the app, go to Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used to purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Cancel anytime in your iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → Nuzzle → Cancel Subscription.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Privacy & Data → Export Data. You can export as CSV, JSON, or PDF.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Privacy & Data → Delete Account. All data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices and never share your data with advertisers.

Can I use Nuzzle offline?

Yes! Nuzzle works offline. Your data is stored locally and syncs when you're back online.

Is Nuzzle a medical device?

No. Nuzzle is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. All features are informational only. Always consult your pediatrician for medical guidance.

Technical Support

If you're experiencing technical issues:

  • Make sure you're running the latest version of the app
  • Try restarting the app
  • Check your internet connection
  • Contact support@nuzzle.app with details about the issue
