# App Store Screenshot Guide

## Required Sizes
- iPhone 6.7" Display (1290 x 2796) - iPhone 15 Pro Max
- iPhone 6.5" Display (1284 x 2778) - iPhone 14 Plus
- iPad Pro 12.9" (2048 x 2732)

## Screenshots to Capture (6-8 screens)

### 1. Home Screen with Daily Summary
- **Flow**: Launch app → Home tab
- **Show**: Baby card, daily summary, quick action buttons
- **Caption**: "Track feeding, sleep, diapers and more in seconds"

### 2. Quick Log in Action
- **Flow**: Tap bottle icon → select amount → save
- **Show**: Quick log modal with haptic feedback
- **Caption**: "Log activities with one tap"

### 3. AI Nap Predictions
- **Flow**: Home → Scroll to predictions card
- **Show**: Next nap prediction with confidence indicator
- **Caption**: "AI-powered nap predictions based on your baby's patterns"

### 4. Timeline/History View
- **Flow**: History tab → Today's timeline
- **Show**: Vertical timeline with multiple log entries
- **Caption**: "Beautiful timeline of your baby's day"

### 5. Charts & Insights
- **Flow**: Charts tab → Sleep patterns
- **Show**: Sleep duration chart over 7 days
- **Caption**: "Visualize patterns and trends"

### 6. AI Assistant Chat
- **Flow**: Assistant tab → Ask a question
- **Show**: Conversation with AI giving advice
- **Caption**: "24/7 AI parenting assistant for questions"

### 7. Paywall (Optional)
- **Flow**: Settings → Upgrade to Pro
- **Show**: Feature comparison
- **Caption**: "Unlock advanced insights and AI features"

### 8. Dark Mode (Optional)
- **Flow**: Settings → Appearance → Dark
- **Show**: Home screen in dark mode
- **Caption**: "Beautiful in light or dark mode"

## Localization
- Capture in English first
- Consider Spanish, French, German for major markets

## Tools
- Built-in iOS Simulator screenshot (Cmd+S)
- Or use Fastlane Snapshot for automation
